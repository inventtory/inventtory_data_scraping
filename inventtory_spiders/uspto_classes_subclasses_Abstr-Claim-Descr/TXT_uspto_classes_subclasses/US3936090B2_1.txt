abstract: 
Inner and outer reinforcing and shock absorbing members are mounted on the
     inner panel of a vehicle door. The inner member is designed to provide
     only low resistance to a lateral impact force caused by a lateral
     collision and the outer member is designed to provide high compression
     resistance in a fore-and-aft collision of a vehicle.
